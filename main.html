Current Projects:

    RSA Data Security RC5 Challenges

    RSA has offered cash prizes for brute forcing cyphertext encrypted with various keylengths). UF Crypto is currently trying to win the RC564 contest.

    We're working with a group that's coordinating an attack against the RC564 challenge called Distributed.net. We're focusing most of our resources on contributing to their effort. Due to the much higher keyrates and better odds of winning provided with the distributed.net clients, we're running them wherever possible. However, d.net won't release their source code. So, we've written our own attack code and we're randomly attacking the challenge with it on systems that we don't feel comfortable running code we didn't compile.
